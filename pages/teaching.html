高效能科學計算暨高能物理使用群教育訓練工作坊 2023

User Training Workshop for NSTCCore & HEP Computing Service 2023

November 15, 2023

此次活動由中研院物理所高能物理與科學計算技術中心 (ASGC) 主辦，旨在提供高效能、高可擴充性與穩定的大數據分析環境的科學計算環境。

我在此次活動中演示了如何充分利用中研院提供的計算資源進行數據處理的平行化。 透過將Dask的HTCondorCluster作為排程器，與RDataFrame (由ROOT CERN提供的數據分析的工具，亦支援Apache Spark做為排程器)結合，以進行平行化的數據處理並將結果視覺化呈現。我也介紹了RDataFrame在C++及Python的基礎使用方法去做了簡單的\(Z\rightarrow\mu\mu\)的物理分析，如粒子判定及挑選，事件挑選等等。

This event was hosted by the ASGC of the Institute of Physics, Academia Sinica, aiming to provide a scientific computing environment with high performance, high scalability and stable big data analysis environment.

During this event, I demonstrated how to utilize the computing resources provided by Academia Sinica for parallelized data processing efficiently. By using Dask's HTCondorCluster as the scheduler and integrating it with RDataFrame (a data analysis tool provided by ROOT CERN, which also supports Apache Spark as a scheduler), I processed data distributively and visualized the results. Additionally, I introduced the basic usage of RDataFrame in both C++ and Python for performing simple physics analysis, such as particle identification, event selection, and more, using the example of \(Z\rightarrow ee\) decay.

實驗方法及物理 (中央物理)

Experimental Methods and Experimental Physics (NCU Physics)

這門課是中央物理大一大二學生負擔最重的課程，其內容包括了電子電路 (RC, RLC, diode, transistor, operational amplifier等等)、物理模擬、文獻調查和實驗架設建構與量測分析等等，目的是培養學生能有獨立完成研究的能力。我也曾是這門課的學生之一，因此我更能了解學生會遇到哪些問題並能夠準備相關的資料來輔助他們。

我曾兩次擔任郭家明老師實驗課的助教。在這門課中，我教導學生使用 Python 語言，使他們能夠靈活運用numpy、pandas、scipy及matplotlib等函式庫來處理實驗數據，包括讀取、擬合和繪圖。在數值模擬方面，我教導了Runge-Kutta method，這讓學生在力學實驗的模擬中更加得心應手。同時，我指導學生在 Raspberry Pi 上使用 Linux 系統及相關的 Shell 指令，並透過腳本來控制各種模組進行測量。我也密切關注學生的學習進度，從課前預習、文獻調查、實驗議題的訂定、到實驗進行，確保他們能夠順利完成實驗。最後他們也都順利的將專題結果發表在中央物理小年會。

This course is the most challenging one for first and second-year students majoring in Physics at NCU. Its content covers electronic circuits (RC, RLC, diode, transistor, operational amplifier, etc.), physics simulations, paper review, the construction of experimental setup and the analysis of measurements, aiming to develop students' ability to make research independently. I have been a student in this course, so I can understand the difficulties that students may encounter and can prepare the relevant materials to assist them.

I have served as a teaching assistant for Professor Chia-Ming Kuo's experimental class twice. In this course, I taught students the Python language, enabling them to utilize libraries such as numpy, pandas, scipy, and matplotlib to process experimental data, including reading, fitting, and plotting. In terms of numerical simulation, I instructed the use of the Runge-Kutta method, which made students more adept at simulating mechanics experiments. Additionally, I guided students in using the Linux system on Raspberry Pi and related Shell commands, controlling various modules for measurements. I also closely monitored students' learning progress, from class preview, paper review, experimental setup, to performing experiments, ensuring that they could successfully complete the experiments. In the end, they all presented their project results at NCU Annual meeting.
