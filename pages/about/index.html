簡介 Introduction

平常喜歡在家裡聽著音樂組著鋼彈模型。想運動時就會跑去棒壘球打擊場打球或是揪朋友去打個籃球。

The experiences gained from international experiments not only sharpened my communication and problem-solving skills but also equipped me with the ability to address highly technical and challenging problems quickly.
