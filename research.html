Search for a Higgs boson decaying into \(\gamma^*\gamma\rightarrow\ell\ell\gamma\) in proton-proton collisions at \(\sqrt{s} = 13\) TeV

碩班期間，我進行了尋找希格斯玻色子衰變為一個光子和一個虛光子，後者再轉化為一對重建質量\(m_{\ell\ell} < 50\) GeV的輕子對的研究。這種衰變被稱為 Higgs Dalitz decay. 這項研究所使用的數據來自於位於大型強子對撞機（LHC）上的緊湊渺子線圈（CMS）探測器在 13 TeV 的質心能量下進行的質子-質子碰撞，覆蓋完整的Run2實驗數據擷取期間，收集到的總亮度為為 138 \(fb^{-1}\)。

這項分析主要聚焦於兩個頻道：電子頻道和渺子頻道。我的碩士論文具體探討了電子頻道。相較於先前的分析，我開發了專門的電子識別和能量校準模型，基於機器學習技術以進一步提高靈敏度。為了提高模型的性能，我用Tree-Structured Parzen Estimator（TPE）算法對超參數進行了優化。同時，還利用SHapley Additive exPlanations（SHAP）值來估計訓練特徵的重要性。

我在這項分析中擔任了主要貢獻者的角色，處理了CMS物理分析小組召集人和多位學著者提出的問題和建議。

此外，我還使用C++構建了統計分析框架，加入了並行計算，大幅減少了數據處理時間。這個框架會自動對要處理的資料進行整合及清理資料(處理缺失資料、資料錯誤、預設值)，應用事先建模(ML)的模型，最後提取目標資料集並視覺化。其用戶友好和易於修改的特性也使得後續的分析人員能夠更有效地利用這個框架。

I performed a search for the decay of a Higgs boson into a real and a virtual photon, where the virtual photon convert into a lepton pair with an invariant mass \(m_{\ell\ell} < 50\) GeV. This decay is so-called Higgs Dalitz decay. The data utilized for this study was recorded by the Compact Muon Solenoid (CMS) detector at the Large Hadron Collider (LHC) from proton-proton collisions with a center-of-mass energy of 13 TeV during the full Run2 period, corresponding to an integrated luminosity of 138 \(fb^-1\).

This analysis primarily focuses on two channels: the electron channel and the muon channel. My master's thesis specifically delves into the electron channel. Compared to the previous analysis, specialized electron identification and energy calibration were developed, based on the machine learning techniques to enhance the sensitivity further. To improve the performance of the machine learning models, hyper-parameters are optimized using Tree-Structured Parzen Estimator (TPE) algorithm. The feature importance is also estimated by the SHapley Additive exPlanations (SHAP) value.

I served as the main contributor to this analysis to tackle the concerns and recommendations from the Physics Analysis Group conveners and collaborators.

In addition, I constructed the statistical analysis framework using C++ for this analysis, adding parallel computing to reduce data processing time significantly. Its user-friendly and easily modifiable features also enable subsequent analysts to utilize the framework more effectively.

Efficiency measurements of electron veto in the e photon identification for the CMS detector.

這個分析是我大學時期的專題研究。我確立了估計這項測量中的統計和系統誤差的方法並量測不同電子阻隔的算法效率(electron veto efficiency) 在光子判定的的影響。這些結果獲得了 CMS 電子/光子物理對象組（EGamma POG）的認可，並被收錄於CMS EGM-17-001論文中，最後刊登在JINST。

This analysis is my undergraduate project. I established the way to estimate the statistical and systematic uncertainties in this measurement. The results were approved by CMS Electron/Photon Physics Object Group(EGamma POG) and included in CMS EGM-17-001 paper published on JINST.
